

# Hapyjo — Premium B2B Marketing Website

## Overview
A static, single-page marketing website for HapyJo Ltd that positions the company as a premium, trustworthy partner for site operations, fleet management, vehicle & machinery rentals, and financial control in Rwanda (RWF). The design will be lavish, classic, and posh — warm creams, deep navy, gold accents, strong serif headlines, generous whitespace, and subtle motion.

## Design System
- **Typography**: Serif headings (Playfair Display) + clean sans-serif body (Inter)
- **Colors**: Off-white/cream backgrounds (#F8F6F3), deep navy (#1A1D29) for text, warm gold (#C9A227) for accents, with optional deep green for secondary touches
- **Layout**: Clean grid, generous whitespace, max ~70ch text blocks, full-width hero
- **Motion**: Gentle fade-in on scroll, subtle hover lifts on cards, gold underline effects

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Navigation
- Sticky top nav with Hapyjo wordmark, section links (About, Services, Rentals, Contact), and a gold "Contact Us" CTA button
- Mobile: clean hamburger menu

### 2. Hero
- Large serif headline communicating site operations + fleet + financial control
- Subline referencing vehicle & machinery rentals
- Single gold CTA button ("Explore Our Services" or "Contact Us")
- Calm background with subtle gradient or refined imagery overlay

### 3. Who We Are / About
- Short, confident narrative about Hapyjo as Rwanda's partner for site operations and financial control
- Mentions vehicle & machinery rentals as core offering
- Professional, trustworthy tone

### 4. What We Do / Services
Six service blocks presented as elegant cards:
- Site operations & field management
- Fleet & vehicle management
- Vehicle & machinery rentals
- Expenses, fuel & cost control
- Surveys, volume & revenue visibility
- Financial control & reporting (RWF)

Each card: icon, title, short description, subtle hover effect

### 5. Vehicle & Machinery Rentals (Dedicated Block)
- Prominent subsection highlighting rental of trucks, earthmoving & construction equipment
- Flexible terms (day/hour), professional fleet, suitable for construction & logistics
- Visually distinct — slightly different background or layout to draw attention

### 6. Why Hapyjo / Trust
- 3–4 short trust points: precision, local expertise (Rwanda), RWF-based operations, professional standards
- Clean layout with icons or small stat-style callouts

### 7. Contact / Next Step
- Company name, optional address placeholder, email placeholder
- Static contact form (name, email, message) styled for later integration
- No app store links

### 8. Footer
- Dark navy background
- Company name + short tagline
- Section links (About, Services, Rentals, Contact)
- © HapyJo Ltd line

## Technical Approach
- Fully static React SPA (no backend, no database, no auth)
- Responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Semantic HTML, proper heading hierarchy, accessible contrast
- Subtle scroll-triggered animations using CSS/intersection observer
- Optimized for fast load — no heavy scripts or media
